Who
Are We?

We are The Engrammers—a collective of ambitious young researchers from the IASDO program at the Faculty of Sciences, Meknes.

Our identity is rooted in the Université Moulay Ismaïl. We are a cohort of students who found a common passion in the complexity of artificial brains and the rigor of mathematical optimization.

We are more than a class; we are an intellectual brotherhood. Each member brings a unique perspective to the table, from pure algorithmic theory to hands-on neural implementation.

Spirit

Youthful Ambition

We reject the status quo. Our energy is directed at solving the industry's most stagnant problems through fresh, AI-driven perspectives.

Core

Hyper-Specialized

Trained in the convergence of AI and OR. We navigate the search space of decision science with surgical precision.

Unity

Shared Growth

We believe that a single neuron is weak, but a synchronized network is unstoppable. We grow together, or not at all.

Our Trajectory.

What We Want To Do

Network Expansion

We aim to build the strongest professional network in Meknes and beyond. By connecting our cohort with industry experts on LinkedIn, we are bridging the gap between academic brilliance and industrial reality.

We are actively looking for peers and mentors who share our passion for optimal decision systems to join our ecosystem.

Knowledge Proliferation

Our mission is to broadcast our learning journey. We take the complex modules from our IASDO program and transform them into accessible articles, GitHub repositories, and visual case studies.

We want to be the primary source for anyone looking to understand the real-world application of Linear Programming and Deep Learning.
